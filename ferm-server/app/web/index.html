Settings

Advanced

Tuning for the control loop and safety backstops. Bad values are clamped to safe ranges on the device. Hysteresis is the dead-band that stops the heater and humidifier short-cycling. Fan after heat keeps the fan at 'Fan: heating %' for a while after the heater turns off, so leftover heat is spread instead of pooling. Max heat caps how long the heater may run continuously before a forced heat cooldown kicks in.

AUTO fan speeds. The fan only recirculates air inside the chamber, so in AUTO it runs the whole time and the speed follows what is being conditioned: full while the heater runs (distribute heat), a bump while the humidifier runs (spread moisture), and a quiet default floor otherwise just to keep the air mixed. A manual fan setting overrides these.
